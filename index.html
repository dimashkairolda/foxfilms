About Us

Fox Films

Fox Films was founded in 2006 by a team of professionals in the field of photography and video shooting. At first glance, simple but creative ideas can be easily combined with style, taste and detail, which allow studio artists to create leading works in Kazakhstan, CIS countries and Europe. We are ready for bold decisions that have not been made yet, and with us - ideas will easily come true! We create masterpieces that bring joy to people!

Ivan Petrov, videographer, founder

Fox Films

Each of our work is a real art shot. We responsibly approach our creativity, working through each step step by step and always investing something new. We have a rich experience of filming around the world, so the geography of our work has no boundaries. Today Fox Films is a large creative association that provides services for creating photo and video stories about your wedding, joyful events and family celebrations, because every moment of life is unique and it needs to be preserved!

Dmitriy Rogov, photographer, co-founder

Fox Films

We have already shot over 500 wedding and family events, we do not stop at the achieved results and are proud of them. In addition, Fox Films creates unique videos that will save the best memory of what happened in life, thereby giving you and your loved ones warm and unforgettable emotions!

Oleg Smirnov, videographer
